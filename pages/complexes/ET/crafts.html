SpaceCrafts

Is ET among us?

Speculation of space technologies has been a topic of generations. From the Roswell incidents in July of 1947 all the way back to spinning discs of Ezekial, humans have argued if we're alone in the universe.

Pentagon

The Pentagon has officially released videos called "FLIR", "GIMBAL", & "GOFAST". They're all grainy, monochromic, and have been widely speculated to contain proof of Alien SpaceCrafts. These crafts show no visual signs of propulsion. They could be Alien crafts, they could be drones... Either way, they've invaded multiple government airspaces to the point that pilots have reported them potentially being a hazard in the sky. Whether it's a black-budget government agency working on futuristic technologies or ET is trying to phone home, we should probably get some answers..
